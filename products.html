Our Product

Chiller

The chiller is a machine built for the purpose of storage of difference produce or product whose storage or preservation life span increase when stored between (+5ºc to -5ºc) basically it removes heat from the produce.

Please note that we have our own standard sizes of chillers and we also build to customers specifications.

You can kindly find the price list of our chillers in the attached folder also please note that prices changes base on client location as other factors need to be considered.

Blast Freezer

This is a machine specifically built for freshly produce such as ,meat(depending on its time of use), chickens, turkeys etc. this machine operates at -35ºc or below. This machine is mostly required by farmers who are looking to go into processing and carry out a large number of freshly slaughter birds.

Which are required to be fast freeze within 4- 5hours before being moved to the COLDROOM for temporary/permanent storage.

Conternerized Cold Storages

This is specially design COLDROOM with blasting effect that is a coldroom capable of performing the functions of a blast freezer this machine is design for the purpose of those who cannot afford to buy both the blast freezer and a coldroom together especially those into processing of chicken we advice them to get this particular machine, also we have clients who are into the supply of frozen drinks or bottle water we also advice them to get this machine as it serves as a multipurpose machine.

Container Coldroom

We also insulate containers conveniently and convert them into cold room or chillers depending on the client choice of machine .

Container Coldroom

Mobile coldrooms can also be built and deployed to client choice of destination this type of coldroom attracts additional cost as the materials used for the framing of its external edges are different from the aluminum angles used for the normal standard coldroom. This type of coldroom/ blast freezer is very efficient as it can also be moved from on destination to another. If the clients needs to change its location, it prevent any need for disassemble and incurring further cost on assembly and disassembly.
